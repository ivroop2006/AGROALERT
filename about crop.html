Crop Price Alert System - Information

About the App

Crop Price Alert System is a digital platform designed to help farmers check current crop prices in different markets. The system also sends alerts when crop prices increase or decrease so farmers can sell their crops at the best price.

Main Features

  • Check daily crop prices
  • Receive crop price alerts
  • Profit calculator for farmers
  • Easy registration and login
  • User profile management

How It Works

Farmers select their crop and market. The system checks the latest market price and displays it on the screen. Farmers can also set a price alert. When the crop price reaches the selected value, the system sends a notification.

Benefits for Farmers

  • Helps farmers sell crops at the right time
  • Reduces dependency on middlemen
  • Provides transparent market information
  • Improves farmer profit
